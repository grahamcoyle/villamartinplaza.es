The Plaza is delightfully decorated in the traditional Spanish style with blue and white tiles, palms, and a central fountain. Originally built as an aparthotel very few properties here have 2 bedrooms and less than half have the desireable views of the internal plaza with even fewer having South-facing balconies to soak up the sun all day. This one has it all so don't miss out!

The plaza was built in the ealy 1970s before Villamartín became well developed and so enjoys prime position alongside the golf course. The top 2 floors remain as residential apartments with the lower 2 floors now bars, restaurants and boutiques making the ideal destination.

Beautifully lit, it is a wonderful place to be at night, the perfect venue for wining and dining on those warm Spanish evenings! To top it all off, this apartment has been fully renovated internally in contemporary style, check out the photos.
